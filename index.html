India-UK Free Trade Agreement Finalized

Trade Policy High Impact
The trade deal will make British Scotch whisky and cars cheaper in India while reducing duties on Indian garments and leather products. Bilateral trade projected to reach $120B by 2030. Indian workers in UK get 3-year social security exemption.
Person 1 Insight: The deal's true test will be how UK's Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism is handled for Indian exports.
Person 2 Insight: Benefits to Indian companies and employees expected to exceed ₹4,000 crore, especially in IT sector.

MUFG in Talks to Buy 19% Stake in HDFC's NBFC Arm

Banking High Impact
Mitsubishi UFJ Financial Group in advanced talks to acquire 19% stake in HDB Financial Services for ~₹12,000 crore. Valuation has declined to $8-8.5B from $10-12B estimated previously.

Bank of Baroda Q4 Results

Public Sector Banks Medium Impact
Net profit rose 3.3% YoY to ₹5,048 crore. NIM contracted ~40bps due to elevated deposit costs. Guidance suggests NIM pressure may continue through September quarter.
Person 1 Highlight: Bank has enabling provision to raise capital up to ₹7,500 crore over longer period to support 15% loan growth.

RBI Proposes Extended Call Money Market Hours

Monetary Policy Medium Impact
RBI proposes extending trading hours from 5pm to 7pm to better serve real-time payment systems' liquidity needs. Currently banks face higher costs for after-hours liquidity needs.
